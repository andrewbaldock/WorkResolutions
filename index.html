Be the Change Agent
 

Recent research shows that roughly one third (32%) of office workers acknowledge the need to adjust their everyday behaviors, but 24% haven't got around to it; and 19% lack the motivation entirely.

Here at Mindjet, we're taking the pledge to be the change we want to see in the office. And we invite you to join us. The Pledge: I pledge to be a change agent. I will be the difference I want to see in the workplace. I will encourage my colleagues to make small changes to their everyday routine so that together, we can push our organization forward. I will start today.
